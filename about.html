About us

Car Charging offers a comprehensive suite of residential, commercial & rapid electric vehicle charging solutions & accessories.

Car Charging is unbiased in the selection of components and systems adding flexibility to ensure cost effective and ‘fit for purpose’ solutions.

Car Charging is brought to you by Energy Systems & Services (energyservices.com.au) founded by Lincoln Young & associates in 2000.

Exponential Growth

The business grew rapidly from Energy Management Systems to Power Quality, Compliance Auditing, Electrical System Testing & Electrical Asset Maintenance and commercial electrical projects, being on the BRW Fast 100 Companies, winning a Green Globe Award and industry awards such as the National Electrical & Communications Association Awards for large Commercial Projects, both nationally and in NSW.

This evolution coupled with technical excellence and project delivery has earned ESS an excellent reputation for quality amongst a varied range of clients from commercial buildings, hospitals, data centres, Telecommunications Operation Centres, shopping centres, banks and specialty facilities such as The Sydney Opera House, State Library of NSW, The Art Gallery of NSW, The National Gallery of Australia, The Australian War Memorial and complex high profile precincts such as Barangaroo in Sydney that including residential towers, commercial spaces, retail and restaurants.

Based on our technical skills and passion for sustainability and renewable energy we identified a business opportunity that creates new revenue stream for our innovative company.
