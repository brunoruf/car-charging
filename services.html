Solutions

Residential

Car Charging offers you an extensive range of Electric Vehicle Charging Stations for your property, we can supply portable EV Charging Stations or supply & install fast home EV charging stations.

Commercial & Fleets

Car Charging installs a wide range of EV charging stations from our suppliers - ABB, Circutor, Delta, Schneider, Wallbox plus our own Car Charging brand are all high quality and Australian certified products. Take advantage of having an EV Charger in your business parking areas, attracting more people for longer, more revenue and also your business related to eco-friendliness!

Ultra Fast Chargers

The ultra fast charger (up to 150kW) supports the quick charge standards of all electric vehicle manufacturers of current and future generations of EV’s that are demanding higher charge capacity than 50kW. The chargers enable simultaneous charging up to 4 cars solving the problem of needing to wait for available outlets. It supports a variety of payment, access systems and is very simple and intuitive to use.

Installations

Car Charging provides licensed, certified and qualified technicians who are experienced and capable to install any EV chargers. The installation for your electric car must be designed to various regulatory and statutory requirements, completed according to the manufacturer’s recommendations and the various State & Territory regulations across Australia.

Solar Car Charging

We work with the best brands on the market to deliver the highest technology at the lowest price. Photovoltaic solar canopy with a charging system for electric vehicles is one of the solutions that we use where it enables the production of energy during daylight hours to cover the electricity consumption of a building and the charging of electric vehicles (EV).

Software

We work with APIs supporting smooth integration with back office systems, energy management solutions and payment services. Car Charging offers advanced web tools to operate and monitor chargers. Web tools analyse the real time status of chargers and configure settings related to authentication. For chargers equipped with a credit card payment terminal, a web tool is available to configure the payment process. All data can be exported.
